Art World

Art World is a virtual art gallery app that allows users to browse and buy artwork from all over the world. The app has an easy-to-use interface that allows users to take a virtual tour of the art gallery, view high-quality images of the artwork, and learn about the artists by reading their profiles. Users can save their favourites, buy artwork directly from the app, and keep track of their purchases. In addition, the app provides resources and information on the art world and collecting to assist users in making informed decisions. Art World allows art enthusiasts to discover and purchase stunning artwork from the comfort of their own home.

The Product

The art world is prepared to offer online purchasing alternatives for artwork and virtual gallery tours. Visitors can easily view and purchase the artworks by using the virtual tour option. It helps those who have hectic schedules and cannot visit art galleries or make the required art purchases, such as businesses and working professionals.

Duration

September 2022 to February 2023.

The Goal

Design an app for Virtual tour app for art galleries – Art World that allows users to view art gallery virtually and buy art work online.

My role

UX designer designing an app for Art World – Virtual tour for art galleries app.

Responsibilities

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wire-framing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.

The Problem

People with busy schedules, such as businesspeople and working professionals, are unable to attend art galleries and purchase the desired art works.

Brainstorming

Throughout the course of the brainstorming process for the Art Gallery app, we talked about a number of features that would offer consumers a compelling and convenient experience. We made the choice to incorporate a virtual tour of the gallery so that visitors could view high-resolution pictures of the pieces and read in-depth explanations of each one. Along with providing a safe payment method and shipping choices, we also wanted to make it simple for people to buy the art they love directly from the app. Finally, we made the choice to incorporate artist profiles that would help viewers learn more about the creators of the paintings and enable them to follow their favourite artists to receive updates on new creations and shows. Ultimately, we wanted to make an app that would introduce people to the world of art and give them a chance to experience and enjoy art in innovative ways.

User Interview

Participants in my interview with potential customers ranged in age from 15 to 45. I found out from the conversation that many people had never been to even one art gallery and were unaware of any current exhibits taking place in their city. When questioned about the reason, they explained that it takes a lot of time to visit art galleries and museums and that they frequently missed events because they were unaware of them.

One of the attendees, who is Student in fine arts, stated that he cannot afford to tour art galleries frequently because they are pricey. He stated that he had to specifically search for each art and artist on Google, which still did not provide him with the necessary information.

Competitive analysis

While looking for ideas and researching other art gallery tour apps, I discovered some that offer some of the same features that I want to include in my app.

The Art Arena is a very old and reputable art gallery. It is located in a trendy neighborhood and in a city with a strong art scene, which makes it more attractive to potential buyers. They have a good online presence and a virtual tour to showcase the art.

The Victoria Art Gallery has a very good reputation in the market for presenting high quality exhibitions. Their online presence is good, and they also offer a virtual tour to present their art work.

The Hayward Gallery is very good at marketing and promotion; they are involved in advertising in art magazines, social media, and hosting events such as artist talks and opening receptions. Their online presence is good, but they don't offer a virtual tour platform.
